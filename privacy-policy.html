Privacy Policy for Kite Glide

Effective date: May 13, 2026

Kite Glide is a casual mobile game developed by Pradip Das Games.

Information We Collect

Kite Glide does not collect, store, share, or sell any personal information from users.

App Permissions

The game does not require login, account creation, email address, phone number, location, contacts, camera, microphone, or payment information.

Local Game Data

Kite Glide may store basic game progress such as best score and settings locally on the user's device. This information stays on the device and is not sent to us or any third party.

Advertisements

Kite Glide does not currently show advertisements and does not use third-party advertising SDKs.

Children's Privacy

Kite Glide is not specifically directed to children under 13. The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

If this privacy policy changes in the future, we will update this page.
